Customer Journey Map Template

A customer journey map is a visual representation of the different stages a customer goes through when interacting with a business, from the initial awareness stage to post-purchase support. It outlines the touchpoints that a customer has with a business, including online interactions, in-store experiences, customer service interactions, and more. The purpose of a customer journey map is to help businesses understand their customers better by identifying pain points, areas of opportunity, and moments of truth where a customer's experience can be positively or negatively impacted. By mapping out the customer journey, businesses can gain insights into how they can improve their customer experience, increase customer satisfaction, and drive customer loyalty.
